An Open English Community on WeChat

Teachers introduce themselves and their classes. Knowledge is shared in the community and spread on WeChat. Students find their favorite teachers and courses. Teachers affect more and more students here.

Groups

We build separate groups on WeChat for every teacher. Students get into the groups if they are interested in the shared information. We will help with the communication with students in the groups.

The official account

It displays the introduction of teachers and their courses. It regularly posts articles to introduce classes, activities, materials, etc. Students go into the groups directly via the introduction and articles. Students make a purchase for the courses that are offered.

How does it work?

Excepts the official account, teachers and their courses are also spread on other socila media. Students will follow the links to the website, and finally find teachers' groups.
